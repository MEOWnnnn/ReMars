🚀 MAIA + ReMars

AI-driven recycling and 3D printing system for Mars.

MAIA – Martian AI Assistant

An intelligent assistant that sorts and tracks waste, optimizes recycling, and supports astronaut morale with creative reuse ideas.

ReMars – Recycling & 3D Printing

A closed-loop system that grinds Martian waste into powder and reprints it into new tools, spare parts, and structures using Binder Jet and SLM technologies.

Website Purpose

This platform visualizes the circular resource cycle — from waste collection to printed output — ensuring transparency, sustainability, and zero waste.
